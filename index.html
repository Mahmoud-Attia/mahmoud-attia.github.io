About Me

M. ATTIA is Chemical Engineer (and soon to defend his PhD), he is currently working as Research Engineer at the French Atomic Energy and Alternative Energies Commission (CEA – Paris Saclay) and pursuing his PhD at the University of Paris-Saclay through Marie Skłodowska-Curie grant in Numerical simulations and Machine Learning for Scientific Computing. He is developing simulation tools and methodologies to study the Gen5 lithium- & sodium-based solid battery systems for electric vehicles. Attia had his master’s in Chemical Engi- neering through ERASMUS MUNDUS master programs as mobility between Europe and USA which granted him double competencies of both simulations and experiments in Nanoscale and Quantum Engineering.
